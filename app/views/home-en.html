Access to the cartography

This tool aims at identifying the research centers and providing an institutional and informational review of academic research in Political Science and International Relations in France.

Developed by the Sciences Po library, it is part of the CollEx - Persée institutional infrastructure (Collections of Excellence for Research) and is being administered jointly with the Association Française de Science Politique - French Association of Political Science (AFSP).

For a more detailed look at the project

Navigate through the research centers in political sciences with the interactive map.

Do your research on this list by keywords. Each center has a detailed record.

All data presented are freely accessible and downloadable in CSV format.

We want to be part of a collaborative and open source approach in order to improve this tool. Feel free to contribute.

Some examples of possible uses:

  • You would like to know which research center is working on the changes of political participation
  • You would like to know which research center could host your thesis project
  • You are looking for the actors and library resources in political science research near you
  • You are looking for the book collections for which the researchers of a center are directors
